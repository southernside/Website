## Company Overview

SouthernSide is an India-based AI education and consulting startup committed to closing the AI knowledge gap in schools and underserved communities. While awareness of consumer AI tools like ChatGPT and Gemini is growing, the deeper understanding needed to use AI effectively, ethically, and strategically remains out of reach for most students — particularly those outside major urban centres.

SouthernSide addresses this gap head-on. Through school workshops, free webinars, institutional consulting, and government partnerships, the company delivers hands-on AI education to students and professionals who need it most. The company also works with businesses and government bodies to design and deploy AI-powered systems — including public-service chatbots with local language support — that make AI's benefits accessible at scale.

---

## Mission & Purpose

SouthernSide was founded on a straightforward but urgent insight: AI literacy is becoming as essential as reading and writing, yet most students in India — especially those in rural areas — have little access to meaningful AI education. Their experience with AI begins and ends with tools like ChatGPT or Gemini, with no framework for understanding how these tools work, where they fall short, or how to use them responsibly.

The company's mission is to change that. SouthernSide brings structured, age-appropriate, and ethically grounded AI education directly to schools and communities — framing AI not as a threat to human potential, but as a powerful tool for amplifying it.

## Current Activities

SouthernSide is actively building its presence and impact through a combination of direct education delivery, community outreach, and strategic partnership development.

### Free AI Webinar Classes

SouthernSide currently delivers free webinar-based AI classes open to students and young learners. These sessions are designed to go beyond surface-level tool familiarity — teaching students how AI systems work, how to think critically about AI-generated outputs, and how to apply AI tools responsibly in academic and everyday contexts. By offering these sessions at no cost, SouthernSide removes financial barriers that would otherwise exclude students from lower-income backgrounds.

### Government & Institutional Partnerships

A key strategic priority for SouthernSide is establishing formal partnerships that provide direct access to schools at scale. The company is currently in active outreach to:

- **Government education bodies** — to integrate SouthernSide's AI awareness curriculum into government-run schools, particularly in rural and semi-urban areas where AI exposure is lowest
- **Little Kite** — Kerala's state-run student technology programme — to co-deliver AI education workshops through its existing school network, leveraging Little Kite's established reach and institutional trust

These partnerships, once formalised, would allow SouthernSide to move from individual school visits to systematic, scalable delivery of AI education across entire districts or regions.
